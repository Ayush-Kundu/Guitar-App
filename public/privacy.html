Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Strummy ("we", "us", "our") operates the Strummy mobile application and the strummy.studio website (together, the "Service"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — name, email address, and password (hashed) when you sign up, or email and name from Google/Apple when using social sign-in.
  • Profile data — guitar skill level, music genre preferences, and avatar selection.
  • Learning progress — songs practiced, chords learned, technique and theory completion, practice streaks, points, and coins.
  • Social data — friend connections, community posts, direct messages, and likes.
  • Device information — a locally-generated device identifier used solely for content moderation enforcement. We do not collect hardware IDs, IDFA, or location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize the learning experience (song recommendations, daily goals, skill progression).
  • Enable social features (community posts, messaging, friend connections, competitions).
  • Enforce content moderation policies to keep the community safe.
  • Improve the Service based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Strummy:

  • Supabase (database, authentication, edge functions) — Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (optional social login) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In (optional social login) — Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API (AI-powered practice assistant) — prompts and responses are not stored beyond the session.

We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account), all associated data — profile, posts, messages, friends, and progress — is permanently removed from our servers.

5. Your Rights

  • Access & correction — view and edit your profile in Settings.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all data at any time from Settings.
  • Data portability — contact us to request an export of your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Strummy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for all network traffic, hashed passwords, and row-level security on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@strummy.studio.
